Cookie Policy

Usage of storage technologies on our website

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites send to and store on your computer or mobile device's web browser when you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, enhance your browsing experience, and provide statistical insights to the site operators.

2. Cookies Used by estar

We use the following types of cookies on this website:

  • Technical & Essential Cookies: These are vital for the website to function correctly. They are used to remember user preferences, such as the chosen language (Spanish or English) and the Hotel Estar custom aesthetic atmospheres (sunrise, noon, twilight, mist), preventing them from resetting as you browse through the site.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: These allow us to collect anonymous data about visitor behavior (e.g., pages visited, duration of stay, general location). This information helps us continuously optimize our layouts and digital guest experience.

3. How to Manage or Disable Cookies

Most web browsers are configured by default to accept cookies. However, you can change your browser settings to reject or delete cookies at any time. Please note that if you choose to disable essential technical cookies, some custom layout configurations (such as saved language or atmosphere themes) may not function correctly.

You can refer to the official guides to manage cookie settings in the most popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Apple Safari
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

4. Updates to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Cookie Policy at any time to adapt it to regulatory changes or technical updates on the website. We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed of any changes.
